All-Day Partygoer Details

The Great Pumpkin Farm is open from 10am to dusk. Our family will be spending the entire day there and would love if your child can too! They are welcome to ride with us there and back.
We have space in our vehicles for 8 kids. Lunch, snacks and dessert will be provided.

Arrive: 10:15am
Depart: 10:30am
Return: 6:45pm

Afternoon-Only Partygoer Details

Because the Great Pumpkin Farm requires a ticket to enter the grounds for the party, we will purchase a ticket for your child and meet them at the west gate, marked Entrance Gate #1 on the map.

Your child is welcome to join us at any time in the day. Official party times are when we have reserved a table and are planning to have team games and birthday cake.

Begin: 2:30pm
End: 5:30pm

Rain or Shine Weather

There are many activities indoors or under shelters but please dress for the weather since there is a lot of outdoor fun to be had too. We will have emergency ponchos in case of rain but raincoats are welcome too.

Allergies

Please let us know if your child has any food or animal allergies. We will be serving things like pizza or sandwiches, various snacks, cider, hot chocolate (if it’s cold out!), cake and all-day partygoers will get to participate in the pumpkin pie eating contest! There are two petting zoos, one for regular farm animals and another for “exotic”.
